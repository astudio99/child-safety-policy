Child Safety Standards

We are committed to protecting children from sexual abuse and exploitation. Our app strictly prohibits any content or behavior related to Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).

Our Safety Practices Include:

  • Moderation of user-generated content
  • Easy in-app reporting of inappropriate content or behavior
  • Immediate action against violators, including account suspension or ban
  • Cooperation with legal authorities where required

Reporting

Users can report any suspected abuse or exploitation directly from within the app or by emailing us at astudio.asan@gmail.com.
